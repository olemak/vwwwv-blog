Set with a heavy hand and a cold eye.

A homage to mid-century propaganda graphics — bold heroic vocabulary stripped of polish — with the weight of a printed page and none of its compromises. Loud where it earns it. Quiet everywhere else.

Section 02 · Type

System body · one display face

Body type is set in system-ui. Reliably reachable weights across macOS, Windows, and Linux: 400, 500, 600, 700. 800/900 work on most modern systems but are not guaranteed — reserve them for the display face. Display headings use a heavy condensed grotesque (Oswald 700, fallback Impact / Haettenschweiler).

Section 03 · Wordmark

vwwwv — palindrome, waveform

The letters form a palindrome and have a built-in waveform rhythm: V·W·W·W·V is six peaks across a flat baseline, like an oscilloscope set to a steady tone. Set as raw geometry, not a typeface — it never needs a font file.

Section 04 · Rules

The double rule, the propaganda signature

A 6 px black rule paired 10 px above a 2 px black rule. Used to open major sections and to close the page. Single 2 px rules separate posts in the feed; a thin red 1 px rule appears only inside long posts to mark a tonal break.

Section 05 · Tag pills

Two variants, no more

Filled is primary — used for the active filter and to mark a post's primary tag in lists. Outlined is secondary — used everywhere else. Both sit on a 2 px ink border. No fill gradients. No hover glow. A 1px translate + drop-shadow on hover signals click without softening the edge.

Section 06 · Figures

One frame, two states

Every image — vintage, screenshot, AI propaganda — sits in a 4 px ink border with a small red caption label below set in the propaganda type. Collapsed: 21:9 crop, title overlaid bottom-left, dark gradient at the foot of the image. Expanded: natural aspect, no overlay, caption only.
